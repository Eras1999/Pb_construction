About The Company

Crafting Sri Lanka's Built Future

The Proficient Builders & Services is a premium construction and engineering company headquartered in Hokandara, Sri Lanka. We specialize in delivering world-class residential, commercial, and industrial construction solutions.

With over a decade of industry expertise, our multidisciplinary team of engineers, architects, and craftsmen brings unmatched precision, innovation, and reliability to every project — from foundation to finish.
